Short Bio

Daiki Kimura is currently a Staff Research Scientist at IBM Research. He is now researching about Geospatial Foundation Model and Neuro-Symbolic AI ( NS toolkit ). He received his D.Eng. from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 2015. During the period, he was also a Research Fellow of Japan Society for the Promotion of Science, a Research Intern at Microsoft Research Asia, and so on. He was a Research Scientist at Rakuten Institute of Technology from 2015. He joined IBM Research in 2016 and became Technical Assistant of Tokyo Lab Director in 2020. He received Best Paper Award, Outstanding Young Researcher Award, Audience Award, and so on.

木村 大毅
